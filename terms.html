Professional workflow and fair business practices

Commission Process

1

Contact & Discussion

Send a detailed message on Discord or Roblox DM with your project details, deadline, and game link if available.

2

Statement of Work (SOW)

I will provide a written summary including project scope and the final price.

3

Payment & Milestones

I use preferred milestones for larger tasks to ensure smooth progress:

Working in Your Group/Project

• 20% Upfront required to start work.

• Milestones are paid as progress is confirmed.

• Policy: No working ahead of the current paid milestone.

Working in My Place/Project

• 20% Upfront required to start work.

• Full playtesting access provided in my personal place.

• Buffer: Working ahead is allowed up to 30% of total payment.

4

Final Delivery & Source Code

Once the remaining balance is paid, I will provide the full scripts/source files for you to use.

Accepted Methods

  • Payoneer: Preferred for larger jobs.
  • Robux: Via direct group payout or game pass (client covers 30% Roblox tax).
  • Others: Discuss other methods (excluding restricted ones).

Important Rules

  • All payments must be made in USD equivalent.
  • Client covers all taxes and fees on any payment method.
  • Strict Policy: No PayPal, no Crypto, no Gift Cards.
  • Non-refundable: Any amount sent is NON-refundable under any circumstances.
  • Delivery: Full payment is required before final files are transferred.

Rules & Terms of Service

Script Usage & Licensing

  • I retain copyright ownership of the core code structures I write.
  • Full Usage Rights: Once paid in full, you are given the scripts and can use them in **ANY** of your projects or group games without restriction.
  • THE BIG RULE: You are **STRICTLY PROHIBITED** from reselling, redistributing, or sharing the scripts as a standalone product.
  • Showcase: I may show completed work in my portfolio or reuse generic logic in future projects.

Payment & Refund Policy

  • Milestones are non-negotiable once work has started.
  • Missing payments result in an immediate project pause.
  • As stated, all payments are final and non-refundable.

Revisions & Support

  • Includes up to 3 free minor revisions for bug fixes or small tweaks.
  • Additional revisions or major scope changes require a new quote.
