Commission Rules & Terms

Professional workflow and fair business practices

Commission Process

1

Contact & Discussion

Send a detailed message on Discord or Roblox DM with your project details, deadline, and game link if available.

2

Statement of Work (SOW)

I will provide a written summary including project scope, final price, and estimated timeline.

3

Agreement & Deposit

Once you accept the SOW, a 30% deposit is required to officially start the development.

4

Development & Updates

I send regular updates and test places. Once 70% of the work is done, a 40% milestone payment is required to continue.

5

Final Delivery

After testing is complete, the remaining 30% balance is paid.

6

License Guaranteed

Immediately after full payment, I grant the official license and deliver the final scripts.

Payment Methods & Rules

Payoneer (Preferred)
Robux

Accepted Methods

  • Payoneer: Preferred for larger jobs.
  • Robux: Via direct group payout or game pass (for smaller jobs).

Important Rules

  • All payments must be made in USD equivalent.
  • Client covers all taxes and fees on Payoneer or Robux.
  • Strict Policy: No PayPal, no crypto, no gift cards.
  • Milestones: 30% upfront, 40% at milestone (70% done), and 30% upon final delivery.
  • Non-refundable: The 30% deposit is non-refundable once work has begun.
  • Delivery: Full payment is required before final files are delivered.

Rules & Terms of Service

Intellectual Property & Licensing

  • I retain full copyright ownership of all code I write.
  • Note: High-quality UI and 3D models shown in portfolio videos are for demonstration purposes and are typically not my own work.
  • Upon full payment only, you receive a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use the scripts in YOUR/YOUR GROUPS Roblox experiences only.
  • Restrictions: You may not resell, redistribute, share, or use the code in someone else's game.
  • I may reuse generic code structures in future projects and show completed work in my portfolio.

Payment & Refund Policy

  • 30% deposit required before work begins.
  • 40% milestone payment due when 70% of work is complete.
  • Remaining 30% due before final delivery.
  • Full payment guarantees the official license and final scripts.
  • No refunds after any work has been delivered.

Revisions & Scope

  • Includes up to 3 free minor revisions and bug fixes.
  • Additional revisions: 1,000 Robux / $3 per round.
  • Major scope changes require a separate quote and agreement.

Timeline & Termination

  • Estimated delivery dates are provided after the deposit is received.
  • Client-side delays automatically extend the project timeline.
  • Either party may end the project at any time; client only pays for completed/delivered work.

Dispute Resolution

  • Disagreements will be handled privately and in good faith. Roblox does not mediate private commission disputes.
